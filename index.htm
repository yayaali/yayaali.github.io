Welcome to Hummus Coffee Mediterranean Restaurant

At Hummus Coffee Mediterranean Restaurant, our priority has always been quality food at best prices and we are dedicated to maintain quality food and service. Our menu selections reflect the authentic Mediterranean food with a relaxed atmosphere which has made us favorite with the local residents. We hope you'll join us for lunch or dinner and become part of our family

This is the house of Egyptian cuasin

The Hummus Cafe offers authentic Egyptian-Mediterranean cuisine. "Eating here is like eating in the kitchen of a fussy Egyptian mom", (City Arts, Jonathan Zwickel). Try our homemade Hummus and falafel. Our traditional green soup is light, fresh & original.
